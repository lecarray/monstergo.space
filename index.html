How to Play

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you must defeat waves of monsters while collecting experience and upgrading your character. Move with WASD or arrow keys and unleash powerful attacks to survive as long as possible.

As you play, you'll unlock new weapons, abilities, and characters. Each run is unique with procedurally generated encounters, making every game a fresh challenge. How long can you survive?

Tips and Strategies

Beginners

Focus on movement and dodging enemies at first. Start with health upgrades to increase your survival time. Don't get cornered by large groups of monsters.

Intermediate

Balance your upgrades between offense and defense. Learn enemy patterns and prioritize which monsters to eliminate first. Collect XP gems quickly.

Advanced

Build synergistic combinations of weapons and items. Kite large groups of enemies effectively and time your power-ups for maximum effect during difficult waves.
